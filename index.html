Pearl Crop, Inc. was initially formed in 2007 under the name of Turkhan Nuts, a family owned walnut and almond company. In 2010, we opened our initial processing plant in Ripon, California at which time we changed its name to Turkhan Foods. Processing both almonds and walnuts and growing rapidly, we acquired the majority of assets and operations of Linden Nut Company located in Linden, California in early 2013. That acquisition allowed us to centralize our walnut processing operations in Linden, leaving Turkhan Foods dedicated to almond processing. In 2015, we expanded our production capabilities even further with the purchase of an additional facility in Stockton, California.
